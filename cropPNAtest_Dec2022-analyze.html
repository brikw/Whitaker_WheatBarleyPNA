Briana K. Whitaker

2022-12-19

  • Run using R version 4.2.1 (2022-06-23 ucrt).

Objective

This document reports on results from the 4th Illumina Nano Run in Dec 2022, on 40 samples. 1 primer pair only. 3 wheat head, 3 barley head, and 3 corn leaf samples times four PNA treatment levels Plus 2 corn debris samples as positive controls of a sort, and two PCR negatives.

Load Packages, set path to data

Summarize & Remove Plant Reads

Run non-hits from DECIPHER on ncbi browser

  • ASVs that are low confidence from decipher output are generally longer than average for regular dataset ~50bp

  • 3 ASVs were Hordeum, while 6 were Triticum, and 1 was a Triticum-Hordeum chimera of sorts. In addition, 6 ASVs were likely chimeras
